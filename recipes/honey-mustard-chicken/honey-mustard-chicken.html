Easy Honey Mustard Chicken Recipe

This is an easy weeknight dinner chicken skillet recipe made on the stove that’s guaranteed to be a family favorite!

Ingredients:

  • Olive oil
  • Boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • Honey
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Yellow mustard
  • Dijon mustard
  • Garlic

optional:

  • Cornstarch
  • Fresh parsley

Steps:

  1. Sauté chunks of chicken breasts seasoned with salt and pepper in olive oil until they’re just cooked though.

  2. While they’re sautéing, mix together the honey and two types of mustards.

  3. Add the homemade honey mustard to the skillet and stir to combine. Voila, the recipe is done and dinner is served unless you want thicker honey mustard sauce.
