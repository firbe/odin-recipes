Healthy Orange Chicken Recipe

The homemade orange chicken is ridiculously tender, juicy, and full of orange flavor.

When I say ridiculously tender, I mean it just melts in your mouth. You’d swear it was fried but it’s not.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breasts
  • Cornstarch
  • Toasted sesame oil
  • Olive oil
  • Freshly squeezed orange juice
  • Lite soy sauce
  • Honey
  • Green onions

Steps:

  1. To a large skillet, add the freshly squeezed orange juice, soy sauce, honey, sesame oil, olive oil, and boneless skinless chicken breast pieces that have been dredged in cornstarch (it’s the secret ingredient).

  2. Cook until the chicken is done, less than 10 minutes, then dig in to this better-than-takeout copycat orange chicken alongside your favorite rice.
